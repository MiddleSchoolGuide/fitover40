# Privacy Policy for FitOver40

**Effective Date: March 31, 2026**

FitOver40 ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the FitOver40 mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection
Your privacy is our priority. Most of your data is stored locally on your device:
- **Workout History:** Your logs, plans, and training levels are stored on-device using a local database.
- **Authentication:** If you choose to sign in, your email address and authentication session are stored locally to allow the app to reconnect to your backend service.

## 2. What is NOT Collected
We believe in data minimization. FitOver40 does NOT do the following:
- We do NOT include third-party ads.
- We do NOT use third-party analytics or tracking pixels.
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties.
- Outdoor location data is used only for active workout tracking and is not shared or stored on our servers.

## 3. Data Storage and Transmission
Account data and workout information are only sent to your configured backend for authentication and synchronization purposes (if enabled). We do not have access to this data unless you are using a service managed by us.

## 4. Data Deletion
You have full control over your data. You can clear all workout history, settings, and local data at any time from the "Settings" screen within the app. Deleting the app will also remove all locally stored data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
