### FitOver40: Strength and Stamina After 40
Reaching 40 is a turning point. Your body needs a different approach to fitness—one that balances intensity with recovery and strength with mobility. FitOver40 is designed specifically for the 40+ athlete who wants a no-nonsense, high-impact training companion.

Whether you're looking to start running for the first time in years or want to maintain the muscle mass critical for longevity, FitOver40 provides the structure you need without the fluff.

### Key Features:
- **Interval Running Plans**: From "Beginner: First Time Ever" to "Advanced: Maintaining Speed," our plans use timed intervals to build your engine without overtaxing your joints.
- **Weight Training Tracker**: Keep track of your progress on core lifts. We focus on what matters for mature athletes: consistency and progressive overload.
- **Privacy First & Offline**: No account required. Your workout history is stored locally on your device. We don't track you, we don't show ads, and we don't sell your data.
- **Health-First Approach**: Safety is our priority. Every plan is built with the mature athlete in mind, ensuring you stay in the game for the long haul.

### Why FitOver40?
- **No Account Needed**: Start training in seconds.
- **No Ads**: Focus on your workout, not on banners.
- **Local Storage**: Your data is yours.
- **Designed for 40+**: Realistic goals and effective programming.

**Note**: Before starting any new exercise program, consult your physician or healthcare provider. This app does not provide medical advice.

Start your journey to better health today. Train smart, live strong.
